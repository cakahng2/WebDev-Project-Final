Education

This is my major.

Have you ever wondered what "BGS" means? Honestly, you probably haven't; you probably have never heard of it, and neither had I until I was referred to the major by an advisor.

"BGS" stands for "Bachelor of General Studies"; it is a major that has multiple emphases, and is the program that I'm in. I am doing the Business Emphasis, and I will be graduating August 2025.

What makes this program so special is that it is an "Independent Study" program. Independent Study programs allow incredible flexibility, and here's why...

In Independent Study, you are completely online. When you sign up for a class, rather than using the traditional semester system, you are given a year from the date of registering from that class to complete it. I will be finishing my classes at a much quicker pace, which offers me even more flexibility than a traditional degree or program.

My path has been awesome, and I'm looking forward to joining the full-time workforce in the tech industry and chip away at my degree while having gainful, full-time employment.
